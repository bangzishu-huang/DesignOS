I enjoy building and designing things. Sometimes they're an app to help with caregiving, sometimes it may be an eight servo robotdog, and sometimes it could be an entirely fake operating system (OS), just like this portfolio. I built this OS because I felt like using a simple Squarespace template would be lying about who I truly am.

I'm heading to WashU to study design and marketing this fall. I'm excited for what the future holds for me. Just like my initial motivation for DesignOS, I hope I will continue to stay curious and build ideas that seem far-fetched.

Well, this ends my journey making DesignOS. For now, at least. I'm sure I'll be coming back with more projects.

Until next time...
